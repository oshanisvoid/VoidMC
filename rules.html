VoidMC Network Rules

  • Be respectful to all players and staff.
  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics.
  • No griefing or stealing from other players.
  • Use appropriate language; no offensive or discriminatory remarks.
  • Follow staff instructions at all times.
  • Do not advertise other servers or services.
  • Report bugs and exploits responsibly.
  • Have fun and play fair!
