VoidMC Network Rules

1. Be Respectful

  • Treat all players with respect. No harassment, racism, sexism, or hate speech.
  • Disagreements are fine but keep it friendly and constructive.

2. No Cheating or Exploiting

  • Using mods, hacks, or exploits to gain unfair advantages is strictly prohibited.
  • Report any exploits you find to the staff immediately.

4. Use Appropriate Language

  • No excessive swearing, inappropriate or NSFW language in chat.
  • Keep the chat friendly and appropriate for all ages.

5. No Advertising

  • Advertising other servers, websites, or services is not allowed.
  • Staff announcements and events are the only exceptions.

6. Follow Staff Instructions

  • Listen to staff members and moderators. Their decisions are final.
  • Appeal bans or punishments through the official channels only.

7. Keep Builds Appropriate

  • No offensive, explicit, or inappropriate builds.
  • Respect the server theme and community
