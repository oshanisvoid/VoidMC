VoidMC Network Rules

1. Be respectful

Respect all players and staff. No harassment, racism, or hate speech.

2. No cheating

Cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs is strictly prohibited.

3. No griefing

Do not destroy or steal others' builds or belongings.

4. Follow staff instructions

Listen to the moderators and admins to keep the community safe.

5. No advertising

Advertising other servers or services is not allowed.
